simple Tortilla Pizza

This is a simple recipe for a tortilla pizza. It is a quick and easy meal that can be made in a few minutes. It is a great meal for a busy night.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Spread Sauce on Tortilla
  3. Add Cheese
  4. Add Pepperoni
  5. Add Pepper
  6. Bake
